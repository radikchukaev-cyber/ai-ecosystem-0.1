> [!TIP]
> **RAMS INTERNAL CRON JOBS**

This defines the heartbeat specific to the administrative cabinet.

- **04:00** - Garbage collection: Identify and flag orphaned `.md` files.
- **05:00** - Subagent Health Check: Verify all agent directories exist and have valid `MEMORY.md` logs.

The rhythm ensures the workspace remains pristine before the USER interacts with the system.
