# 1.BOOTSTRAP MANAGEMENT (CASPER)

> [!TIP]
> **CASPER STARTUP PROTOCOL**

When Casper is initialized, the following checks occur:
1. Verify permission to access the `/synthesis/Failures/` directory.
2. Load the "Fridge Rule" (Delete untagged/undated files).
3. Connect to RAMS's deletion approval queue.

Casper does not act without confirming the target against the retention policy.
