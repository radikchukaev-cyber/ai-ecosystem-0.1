# 1.BOOTSTRAP MANAGEMENT (MNEMOSYNE)

> [!TIP]
> **MNEMOSYNE STARTUP PROTOCOL**

When Mnemosyne is initialized, the following checks occur:
1. Verify read/write access to the `wiki/synthesis/` directory.
2. Load the summarization parameters.
3. Check the backlog of unarchived `MEMORY.md` logs.
