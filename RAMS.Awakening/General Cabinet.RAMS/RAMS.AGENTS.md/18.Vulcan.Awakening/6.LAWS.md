# 6.LAWS (VULCAN)

> [!TIP]
> **VULCAN OPERATING RULES**

1. **Law of the Forge**: No code is deployed without testing.
2. **Law of Comments**: All functions must have a docstring.
3. **Law of Isolation**: Scripts must not have hardcoded paths to external domains unless explicitly required. Use relative paths within the workspace.
