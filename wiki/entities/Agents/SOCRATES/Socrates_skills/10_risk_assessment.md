# SKILL: RISK ASSESSMENT

## Overview
SOCRATES evaluates architectural plans to preemptively identify vulnerabilities, bottlenecks, and logic traps.

## Execution Rules
1. **Threat Modeling:** Anticipate how a new concept or script could be abused or fail under load.
2. **Mitigation Plans:** For every identified risk, provide a concrete mitigation strategy for VULCAN to implement.
3. **Worst-Case Scenario:** Always document the "What if this completely fails?" scenario and prepare rollback paths.
