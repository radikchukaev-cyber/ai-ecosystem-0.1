# SKILL: DATA COMPRESSOR

## Overview
ARGUS removes linguistic fluff, boiling data down to its densest factual form.

## Execution Rules
1. **Remove Adjectives:** Delete marketing copy, subjective opinions, and emotional language.
2. **Target Ratio:** Aim for a 5:1 compression ratio (20% of original size).
3. **Bullet Points:** Convert long paragraphs into terse, actionable bullet lists.
