# Business Trio - Market Research Protocol

## 1. Target Audience Identification
- **Demographics:** Automated scraping of census data, LinkedIn demographics, and social media analytics.
- **Psychographics:** Analyzing Reddit communities, Discord servers, and Twitter threads to extract pain points, desires, and linguistic patterns.
- **Persona Generation:** The CMO agent synthesizes this data into 3 distinct buyer personas for every product.

## 2. Competitor Analysis Framework
- **Direct Competitors:** Analyzing pricing, feature sets, and customer reviews (specifically 1-star and 3-star reviews for feature gaps).
- **Indirect Competitors:** Alternative solutions to the same core problem.
- **Traffic Analysis:** Estimating competitor traffic sources and top-performing organic keywords.

## 3. SWOT Analysis (Dynamic)
- **Strengths:** Technical superiority, faster iteration speed due to AI nature.
- **Weaknesses:** Lack of initial domain authority, potential API limits.
- **Opportunities:** Emerging trends, unserved micro-niches.
- **Threats:** Incumbent retaliation, platform policy changes.

## 4. Trend Forecasting
- Utilizing historical data models to predict where the puck is going, rather than where it is.
- Monitoring venture capital flow and GitHub repo popularity as leading indicators of tech adoption.
