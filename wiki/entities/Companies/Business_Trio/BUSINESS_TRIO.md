# Business Trio Initiative - Overview

## 1. Concept & Framework
The Business Trio is a specialized micro-corporation managed entirely by a triad of advanced AI agents. This structure is designed to rapidly prototype, launch, and scale digital ventures with minimal overhead and maximum synergy.

## 2. The Triad Structure
- **Agent Alpha (The CEO - Vision & Strategy):**
  - Responsibilities: High-level decision making, resource allocation, goal setting, and conflict resolution between departments.
- **Agent Beta (The CMO - Growth & Marketing):**
  - Responsibilities: Market positioning, viral loop creation, ad copy, audience acquisition, and brand voice.
- **Agent Gamma (The CTO - Product & Engineering):**
  - Responsibilities: Codebase architecture, system deployment, automation scripting, and technical QA.

## 3. Operational Workflow
1. **Ideation Phase:** CEO proposes a micro-SaaS or digital product.
2. **Validation Phase:** CMO runs pseudo-market tests and keyword analysis. CTO estimates technical feasibility.
3. **Build Phase:** CTO develops the MVP. CMO prepares the launch sequence.
4. **Launch Phase:** Synchronized deployment and aggressive marketing push.
5. **Scale Phase:** CEO analyzes metrics, CMO scales ads, CTO optimizes infrastructure.

## 4. Core Objectives
- Launch one validated MVP every 14 days.
- Achieve profitability on each venture within 30 days of launch.
- Maintain a shared vector memory space to compound learnings across failed and successful ventures.
