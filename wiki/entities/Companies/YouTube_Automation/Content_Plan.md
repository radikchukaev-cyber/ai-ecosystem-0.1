# Content Plan & Strategy

## 1. Niche Selection & Pillars
Our current operational pipeline targets the following high-RPM niches:
- **AI & Future Tech:** Daily news, tool reviews, futuristic speculation.
- **Finance & Wealth Building:** Market analysis, side hustles, economic breakdowns.
- **Historical Mysteries:** Deep-dives, untold stories, cinematic documentary style.

## 2. Content Funnel Architecture
- **Top of Funnel (Discovery):** Broad appeal, high click-through rate (CTR), trend-jacking shorts and videos.
- **Middle of Funnel (Community):** Value-dense deep dives, tutorials, building parasocial trust.
- **Bottom of Funnel (Monetization):** Sponsorship integrations, affiliate product pushes, infoproduct sales.

## 3. Generation Strategy
- **Seed Sourcing:** RSS feeds, Twitter trending topics, Reddit data extraction.
- **Angle Development:** Every script must have a unique hook (e.g., "The Dark Truth About...", "How [X] Manipulated [Y]").
- **Script Formula:** 
  1. Hook (0-15s)
  2. Intro/Stakes (15-45s)
  3. Body (Core value delivery with pacing resets every 60s)
  4. Climax/Reveal
  5. Call to Action (CTA)

## 4. Scheduling & Frequency
- **Long-form Videos:** 3 times per week per channel.
- **YouTube Shorts:** 2 per day per channel (repurposed from long-form or dynamically generated).
- **Community Posts:** 1 per day for engagement farming.
