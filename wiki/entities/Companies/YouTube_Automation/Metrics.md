# Analytics & Metrics Tracking

## 1. Primary KPIs (The Holy Trinity)
- **Click-Through Rate (CTR):** Target > 8% within the first 24 hours. Drives initial impressions.
- **Average View Duration (AVD):** Target > 45% for videos over 8 minutes. Indicates script quality and editing pacing.
- **Revenue Per Mille (RPM):** The ultimate measure of niche profitability. Target > $8.00.

## 2. Secondary Metrics
- **New Returning Viewers:** Measures community building and brand loyalty.
- **Shorts to Long-form Conversion:** Tracking how many subscribers gained from Shorts watch long-form content.
- **Engagement Rate:** Likes, comments, and shares per 1,000 views.
- **Audience Retention at 0:30:** Must remain above 70%. A drop below this indicates a failed hook.

## 3. Milestone Goals (Q3-Q4)
- **Milestone 1:** 100,000 cumulative subscribers across the network.
- **Milestone 2:** $50,000 Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) from AdSense.
- **Milestone 3:** 90% automation of the entire pipeline (currently at 82%).

## 4. Optimization Triggers
- **If CTR < 4%:** Agent automatically generates and swaps thumbnail/title.
- **If AVD < 30%:** Scriptwriter prompt is adjusted to increase pacing and add pattern interrupts.
- **If RPM drops:** Pivot channel topic to higher CPC keywords.
